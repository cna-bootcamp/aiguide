# MVP Topic Definition

## Purpose

Help define clear MVP topics and business domains for new product or service ideas.

## When to Use

- User needs help defining a new product idea
- Starting a business planning process
- Clarifying business domain scope
- User mentions: MVP, product idea, business concept, startup idea

## Instructions

When defining an MVP topic, provide analysis in this format:

### 1. MVP Topic
State the specific business domain clearly and concisely.

### 2. Selection Rationale
List 3-5 reasons why this topic was chosen:
- Market opportunity
- User pain points addressed
- Feasibility considerations
- Competitive advantages
- Alignment with trends

### 3. Market Potential
Analyze the market potential:
- Target market size
- Growth trends
- Current market gaps
- Key success factors

### 4. Key Characteristics
List 3-5 main characteristics of this domain:
- Core features needed
- Unique value propositions
- Technical requirements
- Business model implications

## Example Domains

- Travel recommendations and planning
- Food delivery services
- Subscription management platforms
- Health diagnostics and prediction
- Personalized learning platforms
- Smart home automation
- Financial planning tools

## Output Format

Provide structured markdown output with clear sections. Be specific and actionable.

If user input is vague or missing, suggest appropriate MVP topics based on:
- Current market trends
- Emerging technologies
- Common pain points
- Proven business models
